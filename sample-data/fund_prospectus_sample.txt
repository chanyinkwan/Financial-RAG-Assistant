HORIZON BALANCED GROWTH FUND
Prospectus -- Issued 15 March 2025
FCA Authorised and Regulated | ISIN: GB00B7H0WQ18

1. FUND OVERVIEW

The Horizon Balanced Growth Fund ("the Fund") is an open-ended investment
company (OEIC) authorised by the Financial Conduct Authority under the
FCA Handbook COLL rules. The Fund is managed by Horizon Asset Management
Limited, a subsidiary of Horizon Wealth Partners LLP.

2. INVESTMENT OBJECTIVE

The Fund seeks to achieve long-term capital growth combined with moderate
income generation over rolling 5-year periods. The Fund targets a return
of CPI + 3.5% per annum after fees, measured over any 5-year period.

The Fund invests across a diversified portfolio of global equities (40-60%),
investment-grade bonds (20-35%), alternative assets (5-15%), and cash or
near-cash instruments (0-10%).

3. BENCHMARK

The Fund is measured against a composite benchmark comprising:
  - 50% MSCI World Index (GBP hedged)
  - 35% Bloomberg Global Aggregate Bond Index (GBP hedged)
  - 15% HFRX Global Hedge Fund Index

4. FEE STRUCTURE

4.1 Annual Management Charge (AMC): 0.75% of Net Asset Value, calculated
    daily and deducted monthly in arrears.

4.2 Performance Fee: 10% of returns above the composite benchmark,
    calculated annually with a high-water mark provision. The performance
    fee is subject to a cap of 1.5% of NAV in any single calendar year.

4.3 Ongoing Charges Figure (OCF): Estimated at 0.92% (includes AMC,
    custody fees, audit fees, and regulatory levies).

4.4 Entry/Exit Charges: None. No initial charge or redemption fee applies.

4.5 Transaction Costs: Estimated at 0.08% per annum based on prior year
    portfolio turnover.

5. MINIMUM INVESTMENT

  - Initial subscription: GBP 25,000
  - Additional subscriptions: GBP 5,000
  - Regular savings plan: GBP 1,000 per month (minimum 12-month commitment)

6. RISK FACTORS

Investors should be aware of the following material risks:

  (a) Market Risk: The value of investments may fall as well as rise. Past
      performance is not a reliable indicator of future results.
  (b) Currency Risk: The Fund holds non-GBP assets. Currency fluctuations
      may reduce or increase returns.
  (c) Credit Risk: Bond holdings are subject to issuer default risk,
      particularly in periods of economic stress.
  (d) Liquidity Risk: Alternative asset holdings (5-15%) may have limited
      liquidity and longer redemption periods.
  (e) Interest Rate Risk: Rising interest rates may reduce the capital
      value of fixed-income holdings.
  (f) Concentration Risk: While diversified, the Fund may hold up to 8%
      in a single equity position during rebalancing periods.

7. DEALING AND VALUATION

Dealing is available on each London business day. The Fund is valued at
12:00 noon GMT. Orders received before 10:00 GMT are executed at the
same-day valuation point. Settlement occurs on T+3.
